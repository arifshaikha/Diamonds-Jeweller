    Navratna gemstones, also known as the Nine Gems or Navagraha Gemstones, hold significant cultural and astrological importance in various traditions, particularly in Hinduism and Vedic astrology. Each gemstone is associated with one of the nine celestial bodies or planets, believed to have a specific influence on an individual's life. Here are the nine Navratna gemstones along with their associated planets.

    Blue Sapphire (Neelam)

    Associated Planet: Saturn (Shani)
    Properties: Blue Sapphire is a deep blue gemstone associated with discipline, focus, and protection. It is believed to bring stability, alleviate obstacles, and enhance mental clarity.

    Ruby (Manik)

    Associated Planet: Sun (Surya)
    Properties: Ruby is known for its vibrant red color and is associated with qualities like vitality, courage, and leadership. It is believed to enhance the wearer's confidence and bring success.

    Emerald (Panna)

    Associated Planet: Mercury (Budh)
    Properties: Emerald is a green gemstone symbolizing intelligence, communication, and wisdom. It is believed to enhance mental clarity, concentration, and creativity.

    Yellow Sapphire (Pukhraj)

    Associated Planet: Jupiter (Brihaspati)
    Properties: Yellow Sapphire is known for its yellow hues and is associated with wisdom, prosperity, and good fortune. It is believed to bring wealth, success, and spiritual growth.

    Diamond (Heera)

    Associated Planet: Venus (Shukra)
    Properties: Diamond is renowned for its brilliance and is associated with love, beauty, and luxury. It is believed to bring harmony in relationships and enhance the wearer's charm.

    Pearl (Moti)

    Associated Planet: Moon (Chandra)
    Properties: Pearl is a symbol of purity and is associated with calmness and emotional balance. It is believed to bring harmony and stability to the wearer's life.

    Coral (Moonga)

    Associated Planet: Mars (Mangal)
    Properties:Coral is known for its reddish or pinkish hue and is associated with energy, strength, and courage. It is believed to enhance vitality and protect against negative influences.

    Hessonite Garnet (Gomed)

    Associated Planet: Rahu (North Node of the Moon)
    Properties:Hessonite Garnet is an orange-brown gemstone associated with protection and strength. It is believed to ward off negative energies and enhance the wearer's intuition.

    Cat's Eye (Lehsunia)

    Associated Planet: Ketu (South Node of the Moon)
    Properties:Cat's Eye is a chatoyant gemstone with a distinct band of light resembling a cat's eye. It is associated with protection and spiritual insight, believed to ward off malevolent forces.
