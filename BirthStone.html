    January Birthstone (Garnet)

    You haven't lived fully if you have not got your birthstone alongside you. It is the month of January, and for January born garnet is that lucky glittery stone you should probably consider having by your side. You might be wondering how this January Birthstone will sail you to places you could never imagine; let us look at it in detail.

    Garnet is the collective name given to a group of minerals. These stones have been around for over 5000 years, with a wide range of colors from red to orange. They are primarily used in jewelry along with other rubies.

    February Birthstone (Amethyst)

    Life is all about adventures. From your birth to your death, your life is an adventure in itself. Above all, if you are born in February, your birthstone is Amethyst - the rare purple variety of quartz that has captivated humankind for millennia.

    Amethyst is the February Birthstone that can be found in the collections of royal families throughout Europe and Asia. It can be cut into many shapes and sizes, manufactured in a lab, and mined. If your birthday comes in February, then owning an amethyst symbolizes personal empowerment and inner strength.

    March Birthstone (Aquamarine)

    There are times when you need to know yourself and know better. Knowing yourself starts from the time you're born before coming to a solid conclusion. Speaking of the time, if you're born in March then aquamarine and bloodstone are two birthstones of march borns. They are very different when compared to other birthstones, but share a similar quality of protecting one's well-being.

    From deep green-blue to light to slightly greenish-blue hues, aquamarine birthstone evokes the colors of the sea. The bloodstone birthstone is typically a dark-green cabochon with the red spots of iron oxide, that brings health and strength to the wearer.

    April Birthstone (Diamond)

    Flying dreams indicate that you could go anywhere, nothing's holding you back, and you have set yourself free. It relates to your personal freedom, control, and unconscious abilities. These hidden talents are now seen in one of the world's most sought-after and adored gemstones.

    May Birthstone (Emerald)

    Time makes us sentimental because it brings happiness and sadness at the same time. In the end, it is our may birthstones that keep us away from all the sorrow and despair. The emerald has that same intensity throughout the world . No one can resist the glamour of the emerald.

    Emerald is a type of beryl with a deep green colour thanks to the composition of chromium and vanadium. It is the most abundant hue in nature, greener than any other color in the spectrum. This gemstone brings a sense of clarity, renewal, and rejuvenation.

    June Birthstone (Alexandrite)

    June, a month of ethereal beauty and enchantment, unveils its treasure trove of birthstones. Alexandrite is the rarest and most trendy June birthstone. June stands tall as one of the select two months graced with three birthstones, each with its charm—pearl, alexandrite, and moonstone. They form a trinity of celestial gems, from the opalescent luminescence of pearl to the milky radiance of moonstone, culminating in the enigmatic, color-shifting magic of alexandrite. Within this realm of gems, those born in June can embrace a gemstone that resonates with any mood or budget, a luminous token of their unique journey.

    July Birthstone (Ruby)

    Learning about yourself, where life takes you, and the twists and turns of your life is good. What if there is a way to continuously control your life to shape it towards growth? Yes! The July birthstone Ruby does exactly that for you.

    The name Ruby comes from the Latin word Rubeus, which means "red." The deeper the red color, the more valuable the gem. Its deep red color is associated with passion and love, which makes it a wonderful gift for all your near and dear ones. The deepest red has a hint of purple and is also called "pigeon's blood."

    Rubies are available from pink to red-purple. The darker, vibrant colored rubies come from Myanmar and Thailand.

    August Birthstone (Peridot, Spinel, sardonyx)

    One of the qualities you must have is to keep the learning thirst going, in every situation. For the people born in the month of August, peridot, spinel and sardonyx are the three birthstones for August.

    The peridot is known for forming under extreme conditions, as it is also found in the hardened lava as well as in meteorites that traveled from outer space. The spinel birthstone is looked as an superb alternative to ruby, a gem with red spinel was mistaken for centuries. Sardonyx is the most preferred August Birthstone, with a history that dates back more than 4,000 years. Let's start the exploration with peridot.

    September Birthstone(Sapphire)

    Some adventures require a willing heart, and birthstones accompany the unique meaning and historical significance and represent the month of a person's birth.

    Sapphire is September's birthstone, coming in various colors, but best known for its blue to violet color, and helps lovers from any harm. It is most desired in its pure, rich blue color but can also be found in pink, yellow, and green; they can also be colorless.

    Natural sapphires are cut and polished into gemstones and worn in jewelry because of the remarkable sparkles and hardness of sapphires.
